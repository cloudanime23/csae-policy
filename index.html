Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

CloudAnime is committed to ensuring the safety of all users and strictly prohibits any content that promotes or depicts child sexual abuse, exploitation, or harm in any form.

Our Commitments

  • Zero Tolerance: Users who violate this policy will be banned and reported.
